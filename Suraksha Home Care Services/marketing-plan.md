## 2. Brand Positioning & Target Audience

### 2.1 Brand Positioning Statement

*"Suraksha Home Care Services — India's most trusted home maintenance partner. We bring professional, reliable, and affordable home services to your doorstep with trained technicians, transparent pricing, and guaranteed quality."*

### 2.2 Target Audience Segments

**Segment 1: Busy Working Professionals (Primary)**
- Age: 28-45 years
- Location: Metros and Tier-1 cities
- Income: ₹12-40 LPA (dual income households)
- Pain Points: No time for home maintenance, unreliable local mechanics, unpredictable costs
- Behavior: Researches online, reads reviews, values convenience and reliability
- Platforms: Instagram, YouTube, WhatsApp, Google Search
- Messaging: "Professional service without the hassle. Book in 2 minutes."

**Segment 2: Nuclear Families (Primary)**
- Age: 30-50 years
- Location: City suburbs and gated communities
- Income: ₹15-50 LPA (family income)
- Pain Points: Home appliances breaking down, safety concerns with unknown technicians
- Behavior: Value trust and safety, prefers AMC/subscription models
- Platforms: Facebook, WhatsApp, Google Search, Email
- Messaging: "Keep your family safe with verified, trained professionals."

**Segment 3: Senior Citizens / NRI Parents (Secondary)**
- Age: 55-75 years
- Location: Any city (children living abroad or in other cities)
- Income: Pension / NRI-funded
- Pain Points: Difficulty finding reliable help, children worry about parents at home
- Behavior: Prefer phone calls, word-of-mouth, WhatsApp for coordination
- Platforms: WhatsApp, Phone, Local community groups
- Messaging: "Your parents are in safe hands. We care for their home like family."

**Segment 4: Property Owners & Investors (Secondary)**
- Age: 35-60 years
- Location: All cities with rental properties
- Income: Rental + primary income
- Pain Points: Tenant complaints, frequent repairs, managing multiple properties
- Behavior: Cost-conscious, value bulk discounts and AMC contracts
- Platforms: Google Search, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, Email
- Messaging: "Complete property maintenance solutions. One point of contact for all your properties."

**Segment 5: Housing Societies & RWAs (B2B)**
- Age: 40-65 years (committee members)
- Location: Gated communities and apartment complexes
- Pain Points: Managing maintenance for entire building, vendor reliability
- Behavior: Formal procurement process, requires quotes and contracts
- Platforms: LinkedIn, Email, Phone, In-person meetings
- Messaging: "Your society's one-stop maintenance partner. Trusted by [X] societies across [City]."

## 4. Facebook Marketing Plan

### 4.1 Organic Facebook Strategy

**Page Setup & Optimization:**
- Page Name: Suraksha Home Care Services
- Username: @surakshahomecare

- Description: 500+ character description with services, USP, cities

- Reviews: Enable and encourage reviews
- Services/Booking: Add services catalog
- Page roles: Assign community manager, content creator, advertiser

**Facebook Post Types & Schedule:**

**Monday — Tip of the Week:**
- Content: Home maintenance tip (seasonal/relevant)
- Format: Carousel or single image with text overlay
- Caption: 100-150 words with bullet points
- Example: "5 signs your AC needs servicing before summer ☀️..."
- Hashtags: #HomeCare #HomeMaintenance #SurakshaHomeCare

**Tuesday — Customer Spotlight:**
- Content: Customer testimonial or review screenshot
- Format: Image + text quote or short video
- Caption: Thank the customer, mention the service
- Example: "Thank you Mr. Sharma for your kind words! We're glad we could help with your AC repair in Gurgaon. 🛠️..."

**Wednesday — Service Spotlight:**
- Content: Deep dive into one service
- Format: Video (60-90 sec) or carousel with steps
- Caption: Explain the service, pricing, benefits
- Example: "Did you know clogged kitchen chimneys can cause fire hazards? Here's how we clean them professionally..."

**Thursday — Behind the Scenes:**
- Content: Technician profile, office, training, team activity
- Format: Photo album or short video
- Caption: Humanize the brand, build trust
- Example: "Meet Rajesh — one of our star AC technicians with 10+ years of experience!..."

**Friday — Offer/Promotion:**
- Content: Discount, combo offer, seasonal promotion
- Format: Bold graphic with offer details
- Caption: Clear offer details, terms, CTA
- Example: "Monsoon Special! Get 20% off on waterproofing services this week only. Book now!..."

**Saturday — Educational/DIY:**
- Content: Simple DIY task homeowners can do safely
- Format: Step-by-step carousel or video
- Caption: Clear instructions + when to call a pro
- Example: "How to clean your AC filter at home (2-minute task that saves ₹1000!)..."

**Sunday — Engagement/Fun:**
- Content: Poll, quiz, relatable meme, community question
- Format: Poll post, image meme, question post
- Caption: Engaging, light-hearted
- Example: "Which room in your house needs cleaning the most? 🏠 a) Kitchen b) Bathroom c) Living Room d) All of the above!..."

### 4.2 Facebook Ad Strategy

**Ad Campaign Structure:**

**Campaign 1: Lead Generation (Always On — 60% of budget)**
- Objective: Leads
- Format: Instant Forms
- Target: Homeowners, 25-55, major cities
- Creative: "Get Free Quote" + service image
- Offer: Free inspection + 10% off first service
- Audiences:
  - Interest-based: Home improvement, interior design, real estate, DIY
  - Lookalike: From customer email list (1% and 3%)
  - Retargeting: Website visitors, video viewers, engaged users
- Ad copies: A/B test 4 variations (problem-focused, benefit-focused, social proof, offer-focused)

**Campaign 2: Service Promotion (Rotating — 25% of budget)**
- Objective: Conversions (website bookings)
- Format: Carousel, Single Image, Collection
- Target: Specific interest + location targeting
- Creative: Before/after images, service demonstration, price mention
- Monthly rotation: Month 1-2: AC Service | Month 3-4: Deep Cleaning | Month 5-6: Pest Control | Month 7-8: Plumbing | Month 9-10: Painting | Month 11-12: AMC/Combo offers

**Campaign 3: Retargeting (Nurturing — 10% of budget)**
- Objective: Conversions
- Audiences:
  - Website visitors (past 30 days, no booking)
  - Video viewers (50%+ watch time)
  - Lead form openers (not submitted)
  - Past customers (cross-sell other services)
- Creative: Social proof heavy, limited-time offers, testimonials

**Campaign 4: Brand Awareness (5% of budget)**
- Objective: Brand Awareness / Reach
- Target: Broad audience in target cities
- Creative: Brand story video, trust building, service range showcase
- Metric focus: CPM, reach, frequency

**Facebook Ad Creative Guidelines:**
- Image/Video: High quality, bright, well-lit, showing real service
- Text overlay: Less than 20% of image area (Meta recommendation)

- Plain text option for personal feel

- CTA buttons: Prominent, action-oriented ("Book Now", "Get Offer")

- Personalization: First name, service history, city
- Send times: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 6-8 PM IST
- From name: "Suraksha Home Care" (not noreply@)
- Compliance: CAN-SPAM, GDPR (for EU customers), unsubscribe link in every email
