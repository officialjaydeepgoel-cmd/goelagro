Our Story

From a Small Dream to India's Trusted Home Care Brand

Suraksha Home Services was born in 2020 from a simple observation — Indian homes deserved better cleaning services. We saw a market filled with unverified workers, inconsistent quality, and opaque pricing. There was no trust, no guarantee, no professionalism.

Our founders, a team of hospitality and technology professionals, set out to change this. We built a platform that combines rigorous professional training, eco-friendly products, industrial-grade equipment, and technology-driven service management to deliver a cleaning experience that matches international standards.

Today, with over 15,000 happy customers across 25+ cities and a team of 2,000+ verified professionals, we are proud to be India's most trusted home cleaning company. But we're just getting started — our mission is to make every Indian home sparkle.

Our Journey

The Suraksha Timeline

Key milestones in our journey to transform home cleaning in India.

2020

The Beginning

Suraksha Home Services was founded in Mumbai with a team of 10 professionals. Launched deep cleaning and regular cleaning services.

2021

Expansion to 5 Cities

Expanded operations to Pune, Delhi NCR, Bengaluru, and Hyderabad. Crossed 1,000 customers and launched sofa cleaning and pest control.

2022

1,000 Professionals

Reached a milestone of 1,000 verified professionals. Launched AC servicing, plumbing, electrical, and carpentry services. ISO certification achieved.

2023

15 Cities & 10K Customers

Expanded to 15 cities, crossed 10,000 happy customers. Launched eco-friendly product line and mobile app. Featured in leading business publications.

2024

Nationwide Presence

Reached 25+ cities with 2,000+ professionals. Launched elder care, patient care, and packers & movers services. 15,000+ customers served.

2025-26

The Future

Aiming for 50 cities, 5,000 professionals, and 50,000 customers. Launching AI-powered service matching and expanding into new service categories.
